About Us

Our History

Started in 2018, Tonk Da Dhaba quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in Ganga Hostel. With its unique brand of world fusion cuisine that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in MNIT. Featuring four of the best three-star Michelin chefs in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The Dhaba traces its humble beginnings to Miss. Tonk Services, a successful chain started by our CEO, Miss. Tonk, that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines.
